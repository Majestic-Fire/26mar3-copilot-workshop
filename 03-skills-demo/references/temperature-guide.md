# Temperature Guide — Food Safety Reference

Safe minimum internal temperatures for cooked foods (USDA guidelines).

---

## Meat & Poultry

| Food                        | Internal Temp (°C) | Internal Temp (°F) | Notes                    |
|-----------------------------|---------------------|---------------------|--------------------------|
| Chicken (whole/parts)       | 74°C                | 165°F               | No exceptions            |
| Turkey (whole/parts)        | 74°C                | 165°F               | No exceptions            |
| Ground beef / pork          | 71°C                | 160°F               | No pink center           |
| Beef steak (minimum safe)   | 63°C                | 145°F               | + 3 min rest             |
| Pork chops / roasts         | 63°C                | 145°F               | + 3 min rest             |
| Lamb chops / roasts         | 63°C                | 145°F               | + 3 min rest             |

## Steak Doneness Levels

| Doneness      | Internal Temp (°C) | Internal Temp (°F) |
|---------------|---------------------|---------------------|
| Rare          | 52°C                | 125°F               |
| Medium-rare   | 57°C                | 135°F               |
| Medium        | 63°C                | 145°F               |
| Medium-well   | 68°C                | 155°F               |
| Well-done     | 74°C                | 165°F               |

> **Note:** USDA recommends minimum 63°C / 145°F for whole-muscle beef with 3-minute rest.

## Seafood

| Food              | Internal Temp (°C) | Internal Temp (°F) |
|-------------------|---------------------|---------------------|
| Fish (salmon, etc)| 63°C                | 145°F               |
| Shrimp / lobster  | 63°C                | 145°F               |
| Shellfish (clams) | Cook until shells open | Cook until shells open |

## Eggs

| Preparation    | Temperature          |
|----------------|----------------------|
| Poached / fried| White fully set, yolk can be runny if fresh |
| Scrambled      | Cook until no liquid remains |
| Casseroles     | 71°C / 160°F internal |

## Oil Temperatures for Frying

| Type              | Temp (°C)    | Temp (°F)    | Use For                   |
|-------------------|--------------|--------------|---------------------------|
| Low fry           | 150°C        | 300°F        | Confits, gentle frying    |
| Standard deep-fry | 175-180°C    | 350°F        | Chicken, fries, doughnuts |
| High-temp fry     | 190°C        | 375°F        | Thin items, second fry    |
| Smoke point alert | >200°C       | >400°F       | Most oils begin to smoke  |

## Oven Temperature Names

| Name           | Temp (°C)    | Temp (°F)    |
|----------------|--------------|--------------|
| Very low       | 120°C        | 250°F        |
| Low            | 150°C        | 300°F        |
| Moderate       | 180°C        | 350°F        |
| Hot            | 200°C        | 400°F        |
| Very hot       | 230°C        | 450°F        |
| Broil / Max    | 260°C+       | 500°F+       |
