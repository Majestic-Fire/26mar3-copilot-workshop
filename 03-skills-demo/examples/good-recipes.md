# Good Recipe Examples

These 10 recipes demonstrate the correct format — proper ingredients, cooking method, and temperature.

---

## 1. Grilled Salmon

**Servings:** 2 | **Prep:** 10 min | **Cook:** 12-15 min

| Qty  | Unit | Ingredient          | Allergen? |
|------|------|---------------------|-----------|
| 2    | pcs  | Salmon fillets      | Fish      |
| 2    | tbsp | Olive oil           | No        |
| 1    | tsp  | Garlic powder       | No        |
| 1    | pc   | Lemon               | No        |
| -    | -    | Salt & black pepper | No        |

- **Method:** Grilling
- **Temperature:** 200°C / 400°F
- **Internal Safe Temp:** 63°C / 145°F

**Steps:**
1. Preheat grill to 200°C / 400°F.
2. Brush salmon with olive oil, season with garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
3. Grill skin-side down for 6-7 minutes, flip, cook 5-6 minutes more.
4. Check internal temperature reaches 63°C / 145°F.
5. Squeeze lemon over the top and serve.

## 2. Roast Chicken

**Servings:** 4 | **Prep:** 15 min | **Cook:** 60-75 min

| Qty  | Unit | Ingredient          | Allergen? |
|------|------|---------------------|-----------|
| 1    | pc   | Whole chicken (1.5kg)| No       |
| 3    | tbsp | Butter              | Dairy     |
| 4    | cloves| Garlic              | No        |
| 1    | pc   | Lemon               | No        |
| 2    | sprigs| Fresh rosemary     | No        |

- **Method:** Roasting
- **Temperature:** 190°C / 375°F
- **Internal Safe Temp:** 74°C / 165°F

**Steps:**
1. Preheat oven to 190°C / 375°F.
2. Rub butter under and over the skin.
3. Stuff cavity with garlic, lemon halves, and rosemary.
4. Roast for 60-75 minutes until juices run clear.
5. Internal thigh temperature must reach 74°C / 165°F.
6. Rest for 10 minutes before carving.

## 3. Beef Stew

**Servings:** 6 | **Prep:** 20 min | **Cook:** 120-150 min

| Qty  | Unit | Ingredient          | Allergen? |
|------|------|---------------------|-----------|
| 800  | g    | Beef chuck, cubed   | No        |
| 3    | pcs  | Potatoes, diced     | No        |
| 2    | pcs  | Carrots, sliced     | No        |
| 1    | pc   | Onion, chopped      | No        |
| 2    | cups | Beef broth          | No        |
| 2    | tbsp | Tomato paste        | No        |
| 2    | tbsp | Flour               | Gluten    |

- **Method:** Braising
- **Temperature:** 160°C / 325°F
- **Internal Safe Temp:** 71°C / 160°F (beef)

**Steps:**
1. Preheat oven to 160°C / 325°F.
2. Sear beef cubes in a Dutch oven on high heat until browned.
3. Add onion, carrots, tomato paste — sauté 3-4 minutes.
4. Sprinkle flour, stir, then add broth.
5. Add potatoes, cover, and braise in oven for 120-150 minutes.
6. Beef should be fork-tender and above 71°C / 160°F.

## 4. Stir-Fry Vegetables

**Servings:** 2 | **Prep:** 10 min | **Cook:** 5-7 min

| Qty  | Unit | Ingredient          | Allergen? |
|------|------|---------------------|-----------|
| 1    | pc   | Bell pepper, sliced | No        |
| 100  | g    | Broccoli florets    | No        |
| 1    | pc   | Carrot, julienned   | No        |
| 100  | g    | Snow peas           | No        |
| 2    | tbsp | Soy sauce           | Soy       |
| 1    | tbsp | Sesame oil          | Sesame    |

- **Method:** Stir-fry
- **Temperature:** 230°C / 450°F (high heat wok)

**Steps:**
1. Heat wok to 230°C / 450°F until smoking.
2. Add sesame oil, swirl to coat.
3. Add carrots and broccoli first (harder vegetables), stir-fry 2-3 minutes.
4. Add bell pepper and snow peas, stir-fry 2 minutes.
5. Pour soy sauce along the edge of the wok, toss to coat.
6. Serve immediately while crisp.

## 5. Baked Pasta

**Servings:** 4 | **Prep:** 15 min | **Cook:** 25-30 min

| Qty  | Unit | Ingredient          | Allergen? |
|------|------|---------------------|-----------|
| 400  | g    | Penne pasta         | Gluten    |

| 50   | g    | Parmesan cheese     | Dairy     |
| 1    | tsp  | Italian seasoning   | No        |

- **Method:** Baking
- **Temperature:** 180°C / 350°F

**Steps:**
1. Cook penne to al dente, drain.
2. Mix pasta with marinara sauce and Italian seasoning.

## 6. Pan-Seared Steak

**Servings:** 2 | **Prep:** 5 min + 30 min resting | **Cook:** 8-12 min

| Qty  | Unit | Ingredient          | Allergen? |
|------|------|---------------------|-----------|
| 2    | pcs  | Ribeye steaks (250g)| No        |
| 2    | tbsp | Butter              | Dairy     |
| 2    | cloves| Garlic             | No        |
| 2    | sprigs| Fresh thyme        | No        |
| -    | -    | Salt & black pepper | No        |

- **Method:** Pan-sear
- **Temperature:** 220°C / 430°F (cast iron)
- **Internal Safe Temp:** 63°C / 145°F (medium-rare) — 71°C / 160°F (medium)

**Steps:**
1. Bring steaks to room temperature (30 min).
2. Heat cast iron skillet to 220°C / 430°F until smoking.
3. Season steaks generously with salt and pepper.
4. Sear 3-4 minutes per side for medium-rare.
5. Add butter, garlic, and thyme — baste for 1 minute.
6. Rest 5 minutes. Check internal temp: 63°C / 145°F minimum.

## 7. Steamed Dumplings

**Servings:** 4 (20 dumplings) | **Prep:** 30 min | **Cook:** 10-12 min

| Qty  | Unit | Ingredient          | Allergen? |
|------|------|---------------------|-----------|
| 300  | g    | Ground pork         | No        |
| 20   | pcs  | Dumpling wrappers   | Gluten    |
| 2    | tbsp | Soy sauce           | Soy       |
| 1    | tbsp | Ginger, minced      | No        |
| 2    | stalks| Green onion, chopped| No       |

- **Method:** Steaming
- **Temperature:** 100°C / 212°F (boiling water steam)
- **Internal Safe Temp:** 71°C / 160°F (pork)

**Steps:**
1. Mix pork with soy sauce, ginger, and green onion.
2. Place 1 tbsp filling in each wrapper, fold and seal.
3. Line steamer basket with parchment paper.
4. Steam over boiling water (100°C / 212°F) for 10-12 minutes.
5. Pork must reach internal 71°C / 160°F.
6. Serve with dipping sauce.

## 8. Deep-Fried Chicken Wings

**Servings:** 4 | **Prep:** 10 min + 60 min marinate | **Cook:** 10-12 min

| Qty  | Unit | Ingredient          | Allergen? |
|------|------|---------------------|-----------|
| 1    | kg   | Chicken wings       | No        |
| 100  | g    | Cornstarch          | No        |
| 2    | tbsp | Soy sauce           | Soy       |
| 1    | tsp  | Garlic powder       | No        |
| -    | -    | Vegetable oil (for frying) | No  |

- **Method:** Deep-fry
- **Temperature:** 180°C / 350°F (oil temperature)
- **Internal Safe Temp:** 74°C / 165°F (chicken)

**Steps:**
1. Marinate wings in soy sauce and garlic powder for 60 minutes.
2. Coat wings evenly in cornstarch.
3. Heat oil to 180°C / 350°F — use a thermometer.
4. Fry in batches for 10-12 minutes until golden and crispy.
5. Internal temp must reach 74°C / 165°F.
6. Drain on wire rack, not paper towels (stays crispier).

## 9. Slow-Cooked Pulled Pork

**Servings:** 8 | **Prep:** 15 min | **Cook:** 480-600 min (8-10 hours)

| Qty  | Unit | Ingredient          | Allergen? |
|------|------|---------------------|-----------|
| 2    | kg   | Pork shoulder       | No        |
| 1    | cup  | BBQ sauce           | May contain gluten |
| 1    | pc   | Onion, sliced       | No        |
| 2    | tbsp | Brown sugar         | No        |
| 1    | tbsp | Smoked paprika      | No        |
| 1    | tsp  | Cayenne pepper      | No        |

- **Method:** Slow-cook
- **Temperature:** 120°C / 250°F (low and slow)
- **Internal Safe Temp:** 96°C / 205°F (for pulling)

**Steps:**
1. Rub pork shoulder with brown sugar, paprika, and cayenne.
2. Place onion slices on the bottom of the slow cooker.
3. Set pork on top, pour BBQ sauce over.
4. Cook on LOW for 8-10 hours (120°C / 250°F equivalent).
5. Pork should reach 96°C / 205°F for easy shredding.
6. Shred with two forks, mix with juices.

## 10. Poached Eggs

**Servings:** 2 | **Prep:** 2 min | **Cook:** 3-4 min

| Qty  | Unit | Ingredient          | Allergen? |
|------|------|---------------------|-----------|
| 2    | pcs  | Fresh eggs          | Eggs      |
| 1    | tbsp | White vinegar       | No        |
| -    | -    | Salt                | No        |

- **Method:** Poaching
- **Temperature:** 85°C / 185°F (gentle simmer, NOT boiling)

**Steps:**
1. Fill a saucepan with water, add vinegar and a pinch of salt.
2. Heat to 85°C / 185°F — small bubbles on the bottom, no rolling boil.
3. Create a gentle swirl with a spoon.
4. Crack egg into the center of the swirl.
5. Poach for 3-4 minutes until white is set, yolk is runny.
6. Remove with a slotted spoon, drain on paper towel.
